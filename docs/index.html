Why Airo TV

Designed around real viewing problems.

Not another stretched phone interface or copied IPTV checklist. Airo TV uses shared modular foundations, then adapts navigation, layouts, release profiles, and qualification to each device class.

One platform, adapted per device

TV, mobile, Cast, and desktop paths share product foundations while preserving remote, touch, and desktop interaction needs.

Available

Qualified at real viewports

The release process defines 1920x1080, 1280x720, 1024x576, and 390x844 profiles instead of assuming one layout fits all.

Under qualification

Built for very large playlists

Local indexing, worker-based processing, and virtualized lists target playlists with thousands of entries without freezing the interface.

The current product

From playlist to playback.

Airo TV keeps the core journey direct. Add a source you are authorized to use, browse or search it, and start watching on the screen in front of you.

01

Bring your source

Add an authorized M3U or M3U8 URL. Airo TV does not sell, bundle, recommend, or unlock channels.

02

Browse from the couch

TV-sized hierarchy, visible focus, and clear categories make the experience readable at living-room distance.

03

Find channels quickly

Search the playlist by channel name instead of scrolling through every group and entry.

04

Play with clear controls

Start supported streams, return to browsing, and use Cast controls where the sender device and network support discovery.

Private validation

Airo Pro explores the intelligence layer.

These capabilities are being validated privately. They are not included in the public Airo TV release and carry no delivery promise until public evidence is published.

The public page describes customer outcomes only. Private repositories, services, implementation details, and commercial rules remain private.

Cleaner imports

Recognize channel variants, merge duplicates, preserve unknown entries, and prefer healthier sources.

More resilient playback

Rank alternate sources and use bounded failover when a stream errors or stalls.

Regional discovery

Explore curated and health-informed rows without implying popularity tracking that does not exist.

Guide intelligence and reminders

Validate bounded programme windows and local reminder behavior before public release.

Richer context

Explore licensed metadata and sports-event surfaces behind explicit data and provider gates.

Trust before features

Your sources. Clear boundaries. Public evidence.

Airo TV is a media player for playlists and streams you are authorized to access. It does not provide, host, sell, endorse, verify, or distribute television channels, playlists, subscriptions, or media catalogs.

Local-first means local where the product can be local. Remote playlists, streams, Cast, and optional future services still use a network, and the page will say so plainly.
