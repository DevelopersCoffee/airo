Trust before features

Your sources. Clear boundaries. Public evidence.

Airo TV is a media player for playlists and streams you are authorized to access. It does not provide, host, sell, endorse, verify, or distribute television channels, playlists, subscriptions, or media catalogs.

The optional website demo is different from the Airo TV app. It connects directly to one third-party public stream only after you press Play, and it may include advertising or regional restrictions.

Local-first means local where the product can be local. Remote playlists, streams, Cast, and optional future services still use a network, and the page will say so plainly.
