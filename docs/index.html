AI-First Built Integration

How Airo TV leverages modern edge computing and on-device machine learning to change streaming

Natural Voice Search

Talk to your TV in natural language. Powered by local speech-to-text, Airo TV maps "find a funny 90s action film with space travel" to channels or movies without sending your voice bytes to a cloud server.

Smart Sorting Engine

Say goodbye to long, unsorted list scrolls. The local AI classifier parses metadata strings, channel labels, and logo configurations to automatically group streams into clean categories.

Privacy-First Architecture

Your playlists, history, and voice inputs never leave your TV. Airo TV runs completely offline-first with no remote tracker APIs, keeping your data confidential.

Offline EPG Hydration

Our intelligent cached program guide system automatically links your channels to schedule XML TV feeds, matching entries based on semantic name mapping.

LiteRT Edge Performance

Highly optimized, memory-conscious engine engineered specifically for Android TV, Fire TV sticks, and low-spec platforms. Keeps RAM consumption below 1.2GB.

Unified Codec Pipeline

Supports all major streaming formats (M3U8, HLS, MPEG-TS, RTMP) and playback extensions with auto-recovery and cast support.
