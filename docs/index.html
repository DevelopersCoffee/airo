Airo TV v0.0.2

Airo TV is part of Airo: a focused Android TV player for your own authorized M3U playlists.

Android TV package

Ships from the v2 release line as io.airo.app.tv with both mobile launcher and Leanback launcher support for Pixel and TV smoke tests.

Bring your playlist

Paste an authorized M3U URL, search channels, and play streams. Airo TV does not provide IPTV content or copyrighted media sources.

Cast controls and status

Includes discovery messaging, pause/play, stop, reload, new session, and volume controls. Cast requires _googlecast._tcp and port 8009 reachability.

Play Store readiness

Category: Entertainment / Video Players & Editors. Release checks cover TV launch, Pixel portrait/landscape fallback, IPTV playlist flow, Music India search/play, Cast diagnostics, accessibility labels, SHA256 checksums, and clean release assets.
